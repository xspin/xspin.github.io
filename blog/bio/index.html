Xiao Cheng

Biography

I am currently a Master candidate in Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University from 2016 and supervised by Prof. Guihai Chen. Before that, I received my B.Sc degree in Mathematics and Computing Science from Changsha University of Science and Technology in 2015. My main research interests include Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) Networks and NLP.
